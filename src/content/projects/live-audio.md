I've been the engineer behind a console for more than a decade — first
in Brazil at festival and tour scale, now in San Francisco on corporate
and broadcast work. The gear changes; the job doesn't. You're responsible
for what the audience hears, and you're responsible for what the artist
hears, and a lot of the time those two answers aren't the same.

## Block Inc. conference, Oakland — Clair Global, Oct 2025

City-wide audio infrastructure for a multi-stage corporate event. Fiber
optic backbone with decentralized speaker systems across the venue.
I was the one engineer on **Stage 16** from load-in through show:
system setup, rigging, cabling, live operation, plus PA tuning and
RF coordination shared across the campus.

## Vocal Livre — A1 and OPS coordinator, 2015 – 2022

Lead audio for an entire band's touring and broadcast operation across
Brazil. Events from 100-person rehearsals up to 25,000-attendee festivals.
Technical advance, run-of-show, FOH and monitor — and the unglamorous
parts: rider negotiation, freight, crew scheduling, the moment-of-truth
phone call to a venue's house engineer.

- **Latin Grammy Nominee, 2023** — part of the production team for the
  album <em>Temporal</em>.
- Opener of the *Por Toda Terra* tour at Brasília.

Not every audio job happens on a stage. Tracking days, broadcast mix sessions,
and pre-production with the artist — quieter rooms, same job: get a clean
recording of what's actually being played.

## A2 at Rock in Rio, 2017

One month, 80,000 attendees a day, supporting senior techs on RF
coordination and stage audio. The kind of show that recalibrates what
"big" means.

## UNASP — AV operations, 2015 – 2020

Led AV ops for weekly broadcasts and live productions with 5,000+
attendees. Trained students on signal flow and broadcast workflow —
some of the best engineers I know came up through that room.

## The kit I'm fluent on

- **Consoles** — Yamaha CL5 / QL / PM, DiGiCo SD / S, Avid S6L.
- **PA** — Meyer Sound (PANTHER, LEO, MILO, Leopard), L-Acoustics
  (K1, K2, Kara), d&b audiotechnik (J, V, Y series).
- **RF / IEM** — Shure Axient Digital, Shure PSM, Sennheiser EW,
  Wireless Workbench.
- **Networking** — Dante, NDI, Crestron, BlackMagic, OBS, Wirecast.
