The van is the project that taught me there is no such thing as a finished project — only a project that's currently good enough to drive away in.

## What's in it

- Bed platform with storage underneath.
- 12V house electrical system (the same automotive-grade-ish 12V world that Shaddow's dock power stage lives in — there's a reason that project exists).
- A workspace that's actually usable for soldering and software work.

## What I learned

Every assumption about "I'll do it the right way later" got tested. The fixes that survived weren't the prettiest ones — they were the ones I could redo at 11pm in a parking lot.
