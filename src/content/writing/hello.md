I'm putting this site up for a simple reason: I've been building things for years and the work has been scattered across GitHub repos, Flux.ai projects, and a van that's parked somewhere in San Francisco. I wanted one place to point people to.

## What goes here

- **Projects** — the things I'm actively building. Right now that's Shaddow (hardware + software for a vehicle dock) and the van itself.
- **Writing** — short essays about engineering decisions, the trade-offs I'm actually making, and the things the van teaches me that no textbook does.
- **About** — who I am, where I am, how to reach me.

## What I won't do here

Pretend the work is finished when it isn't. Most of what I build is mid-revision. I'd rather show that than a polished case study that hides the seams.
